Proceudral Dungeon Generator Prototype

This procedural dungeon generator is designed to create dynamic and replayable environments for players to explore. Each dungeon layout is algorithmically generated, ensuring unique structures with every playthrough. The prototype focuses on achieving balance between randomness and design intent, offering diverse layouts while maintaining cohesive gameplay flow.

Core Features

  • Room Generation: Uses room library with varying sizes and shapes to create variety
  • Pathfinding: Ensures dungeons are fully traversable, with logical connections between rooms and no isolated rooms
  • Enemy and Loot Placement: Populates the dungeon with appropriate enemies and treasures, scaling with difficulty levels.

Implementation

  1. Grid-Based Layout: The dungeon is built on a grid system, ensuring scalability and alignment of rooms and hallways.
  2. Randomization with Constraints: Algorithms generate room placements with rules to avoid overlapping or inaccessible areas.
  3. Testing and Iteration: Prototypes are playtested to refine the balance of difficulty and ensure engaging player experiences.

Player Interaction

  • Players can assemble a dungeon by drawing and placing room templates onto the grid
  • Enemies and Loot are placed into rooms
  • All decisions are made algorithmically according to dice rolls

Next Steps

Future development will transition this concept into a digital format using Unity, implementing procedural algorithms for room generation, enemy AI, and dynamic environmental storytelling.
